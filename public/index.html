Connecting Culinary Minds

The Revolving Kitchen (“TRK”) is a digital marketplace helping food entrepreneurs gain access to commercially certified kitchen spaces, to produce commercially saleable foods, at convenient locations and competitive rental rates within Toronto. TRK achieves this by leveraging existing Commercial Kitchen spaces with spare capacity creating a network of available Commercial Kitchen Spaces. TRK is like AirBnB for Kitchen.

Kitchen Seekers

We support early-stage Food Entrepreneurs like Caterers, freelance Bakers, Food Truck Owners, aspiring Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) Owners, Consumer Packaged Goods (CPG) Food Product Makers, other Food Innovators, and Foodies alike seeking commercial certified kitchen space and provide:

  • Flexibility: ability to search for kitchens based on your custom equipment, location, and time preferences
  • Convenience: real-time listing of kitchen availability in Toronto and online scheduling
  • Affordability: increased kitchen access and competitive pricing
  • Peace-of-mind with respect to kitchen rentals through our trusted marketplace
  • Opportunity: Pathway to setting up a "Virtual Kitchen" and added partner to increase Marketing and Sales
